## The Bracket That Rules America
The NFL playoffs are a single-elimination tournament. Twelve teams started—six from the AFC and six from the NFC. The top two teams from each conference get a "bye"—a week off to rest while the others beat each other up.

### Where We Stand Right Now (Jan 10-12, 2026)

*   **NFC Wins:** LA Rams, Chicago Bears, San Francisco 49ers.

## The AFC: Where the Real War Is

### Bills vs Broncos
Josh Allen is built like a Peshawari kabuli pulao—hefty, filling, and absolutely devastating. The Broncos have a defense tighter than a Pathan's pagri. But Buffalo has nothing to lose.

### Texans vs Patriots
The Texans are "new money"—flashy and talented. The Patriots are "old money" with six Super Bowls. Can the student (Coach DeMeco Ryans) beat the teacher (Bill Belichick)?

## The NFC: Where Legends Are Made

### 49ers vs Seahawks
This is Pakistan vs. India, but with more expensive haircuts. Seattle has the home-field advantage (14-3 record), while the 49ers just knocked out the defending champion Eagles.

### Rams vs Bears
The Rams are Hollywood movie stars—glamorous and expensive. The Bears are the story of the season, rising from the ashes with rookie sensation Caleb Williams.

## The Pakistani Connection: Why This Matters to Us
