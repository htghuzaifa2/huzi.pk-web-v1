## Quarterback Duel: Allen's Spirit vs Lawrence's Near-Masterpiece

**Josh Allen** played with a sore foot and took brutal hits, yet delivered with MVP-level toughness. His dual-threat runs and 4th-down "tush push" gained a decisive 10 yards.

**Trevor Lawrence** had a career year, hitting big throws to **Parker Washington**, but two interceptions—one game-sealing—proved costly. Lawrence was good, but in the clutch, Allen elevated.

## Team Strength Breakdown

### Offense
*   **Jaguars:** Explosive and balanced with **Etienne**, but stalled in the final minute.
*   **Bills:** Relied heavily on Allen's arm and legs (Khalil Shakir catch dominance).

### Defense
*   **Jaguars:** Boasted an elite rush defense but collapsed on the final drive.
*   **Bills:** Forced key 4th-quarter stops and capitalized on every mistake.
