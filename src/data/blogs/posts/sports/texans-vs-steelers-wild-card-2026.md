The Houston Texans' 30-6 victory over the Pittsburgh Steelers (January 12, 2026) was the moment the "H-Town Swarm" turned into a hurricane.

## The Quick Verdict: Houston Rules the Day

*   **The Result:** Texans dominated 30-6, securing their first road playoff victory in franchise history.
*   **The Star Unit:** The Texans' #1 ranked defense held **Aaron Rodgers** to zero touchdowns and 175 total yards.
*   **Turning Point:** A 23-point explosion in the 4th quarter, fueled by defensive scores from **Sheldon Rankins** and **Calen Bullock**.
*   **Key Stat:** Houston converted 10 of 15 third downs; Pittsburgh was stifled at 2 of 14.

## The H-Town Swarm: A Defensive Clinic

For three quarters, it was a 7-6 grind. But in the final frame, the Texans broke the Steelers' will. Sheldon Rankins' 33-yard fumble return sparked the rout, and Calen Bullock's 50-yard pick-six silenced Acrisure Stadium.

At 42, the future Hall of Famer looked every bit a man who had given everything. If a pick-six to a rookie was his final pass, it's a cruel end. The Steelers' playoff drought now reaches nine years.
