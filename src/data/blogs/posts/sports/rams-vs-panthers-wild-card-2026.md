## The Rollercoaster Ride: How the Game Unfolded

The Rams jumped out to a 14-0 lead early, with **Puka Nacua** dominating. But Carolina refused to fade. **Bryce Young**, in his playoff debut, scrambled for a TD before halftime. 

The drama peaked in the 4th quarter when **Isaiah Simmons** blocked a Rams punt, setting up a **Jalen Coker** TD to give Carolina a 31-27 lead with 2:39 left. The stadium was shaking.

## Stafford: The Fourth-Quarter Assassin

With 2:38 remaining, down by four, Stafford stepped up. He completed 6 of 7 passes on a 71-yard march. The "dagger" was a perfectly placed 19-yard strike to **Colby Parkinson**, who toe-tapped the sideline for the lead. It was Stafford's 42nd career 4th-quarter comeback.

## Why This Game Felt Special

Carolina entered as an 8-9 underdog, a "losing record" team that backed into the playoffs. But they played with fire—Young's poise and **Chuba Hubbard's** power nearly pulled off the upset.
