Yaar, if you've ever felt the ache of waiting years for something special, then you know exactly how Bears fans have felt. No playoff wins since 2010—heartbreaks piling up like old newspapers. 

But as I sit here in Sialkot, with the smell of fresh paratha in the air, I can tell you: the **Chicago Bears are alive and roaring.**

## The Headline: A Resurrection in Chicago

The Chicago Bears just pulled off their first playoff victory in 15 years—a stunning **31-27 comeback** over the Green Bay Packers on **January 10, 2026**. Down 21-3 at halftime, they erased an 18-point deficit—the largest postseason comeback in franchise history.

### The 2025 Regular Season Journey
*   **Record:** 11-6 (NFC North Champions).
*   **Coach:** Ben Johnson (First-year HC, former Lions OC).
*   **Identity:** "Comeback Kings" with seven 4th-quarter rallies.

## Caleb Williams: The No. 1 Pick Grows Up

The second-year QB threw for **3,942 yards and 27 TDs** in the regular season, breaking franchise records. In the Wild Card win, he threw for 361 yards and the game-winner to **DJ Moore**. His composure when trailing is now the best in the league (132.1 rating on 3rd down).

## The Monsters of the Midway Defense

The Bears led the NFL with **33 takeaways** in the regular season (+21 differential). 
*   **Safety Kevin Byard III:** Anchored a unit that allowed only 6 points in the 2nd half against Green Bay.
*   **Rookie Impact:** RBs **Swift** and **Monangai**, plus TE **Colston Loveland** (137 yards in the playoff win), have energized the offense.
