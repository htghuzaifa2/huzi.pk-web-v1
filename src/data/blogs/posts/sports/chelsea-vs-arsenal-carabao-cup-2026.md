## The High-Impact Summary: What You Need to Know

*   **The Scenario:** Carabao Cup Semi-Final first leg at Stamford Bridge (January 2026).

## The Rosenior Revolution: A New Dawn at the Bridge

Liam Rosenior’s arrival at Chelsea is a chapter no one saw coming. Stepping into the Stamford Bridge dugout for your first home game against *this* Arsenal side is like climbing K2 without a harness. He inherits immense talent like **Enzo Fernandez** and **Estevao Willian**, but must forge a cohesive soul.

### Chelsea’s Tactical Shape
Rosenior seems to favor a high-pressing 4-2-3-1, relying on the explosive pace of **Pedro Neto** and the youthful magic of **Estevao** to catch Arsenal on the break.

## Arteta’s Arsenal: The Finished Product?

Mikel Arteta has built a machine. The addition of **Viktor Gyökeres** has given them the physical "number nine" they lacked, while **Declan Rice** and **Martin Ødegaard** dominate the midfield. For Arsenal, this isn't just a trophy; it's a statement of treble intent.
