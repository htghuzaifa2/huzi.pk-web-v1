Bruno Mars has announced his first solo stadium tour in nearly a decade: **"The Romantic Tour."**

## The Essential Guide: Dates, Tickets, and Openers

If you’re here to secure your spot under the disco ball, here is the high-impact info:
*   **The Album:** *The Romantic* officially drops on **February 27, 2026**.
*   **Kick-off:** **April 10, 2026**, at Allegiant Stadium, Las Vegas.
*   **Wembley Takeover:** Expanded to five nights (July 18, 19, 22, 24, and 25) due to astronomical demand.
*   **Ticket Prices:** Starting around **$122 (£71)** for general seats.
*   **Special Guests:** Featuring **Anderson .Paak** (as DJ Pee .Wee), **RAYE**, **Victoria Monét**, or **Leon Thomas** depending on the city.
