## The Birth of Dilbert: Laughing at Office Life

Imagine a young Scott Adams stuck in a cubicle at Pacific Bell. He doodled to survive—a mouthless engineer named **Dilbert** and an idiotic "pointy-haired boss." By the 90s, the strip exploded. It was translated into 25+ languages because it spoke a universal truth: **corporate absurdity.** 

The "Dilbert Principle" was known worldwide: *"Ineffective workers are systematically moved to management where they can do the least damage."*

## A Reinvigorated Career and Controversy

In his later years, Adams pivoted to becoming a "persuasion guru" and political commentator. His popular daily podcast, **"Real Coffee with Scott Adams,"** analyzed the world through a persuasion filter. 

However, his journey was also marked by controversy. In 2023, after polarizing comments, Dilbert was dropped by hundreds of newspapers. He relaunched as *Dilbert Reborn* on independent platforms, proving his resilience (or stubbornness, depending on your view).

## Facing Mortality with Grace

Diagnosed with aggressive cancer in mid-2025, Adams faced the end with unflinching honesty. Paralyzed below the waist in his final months, he continued to podcast from his hospital bed. His farewell message was simple: *"Be useful... I loved you all till the very end."*
